ING Zeehelden Eiland

Location: ING Eiland - Orange Ocean

Event type: Culture, Education, Party/afterparty, Bar/restaurant, Music, Nautic, Performance, Tall Ships, Theater

'The heros of the sea explore the world', unforgettable stories of the past but also from the present. Those who are brave enough to enter world get the chance to become a hero of the sea by joining the exiting activities on the isle. You can also visit the Tall Ship the Etoile du Roy. At the end of each day new DJ talents will arrive on the isle to transform the place to a real party haven. You can reach the isle by ferry (or via the Bogortuin).
